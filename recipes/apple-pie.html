Apple Pie

A phenomenal Apple Pie cooked to perfection

I love apple pie, I love the gooey apple centre mixed with the cinnamon

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 Box of (14.1 oz) refrigerated premade pie crust

Filling

  • 6 Cups thinly sliced, peeled apples (6 medium)
  • ¾ Cup of sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ¾ Teaspoon Ground cinnamon
  • ¼ Teaspoon of Salt
  • ⅛ Teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 Tablespoon lemon juice

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 425℉. Place pie crust in a ungreased 9-inch glass pie plate. Press firmly against the side and bottom
  2. In a large bowl, gently mix filling ingredients;spoon it crust lined pie plate. Top with second crust. Wrap excess top crust under bottom crust edge, pressing the edges together to seal. Cut several slits or shapes in top crust.
  3. Bake 40-45 minutes or until apples are tender and crust is golden brown. Cover edge of crust with 2 to 3 inch wide strips of foil after the first 15-20 minutes of baking to prevent excessive browning. Cool on cooling rack at least 2 hours before serving
